Who are the µ's?

They are a group of nine students from Otonokizaka Academy who became school idols to save their school from being shut down. Their purpose was to bring attention to the school. The group disbanded when the third years graduated.

The name µ's comes from Greek mythology's "nine goddesses of music", composed of nine members. Nozomi Tojo was the one who gave the name to the group since she thought it was a fit as they were also composed of nine members.

What is Love Live! School Idol Festival?

This is a social rhythm game for mobile devices (both for iOS and Android) produced by KLabGames and bushiroad. In the game, the player creates units based on collectible virtual cards and performs Live concerts with a designated unit. All the songs used in the game are sung by members of the Love Live! franchise idol groups: μ's, A-RISE and Aqours.
